The Tiers of Being


In this book, (I'm guessing because George thought it'd be funny) chromosomes act as a currency, power level, and duration of time. Very silly.

Ultimate god Literally god, all the power in the universe avaliable to it. kinda looks like a ball of light. Lost all need for chromosomes. There only exists one at any given time
Peace lord Hold exorbitantly high amounts of chromosomes, not necesarrily benevolent nor evil. Extremely powerful, godlike. Hold many different forms.
Chimpilla Though nowhere near peace lord, still hold a sizeable portion of chromosomes, are very powerful and many in numbers. Leaning more towards the evil spectrum
Rankenfile A sizeable amount of chromosomes, large in number and generally somewhat strong. Generally good, evil does still exist in this tier though
Mortal Puny and weak, this is where we reside. Few chromosomes, quasi existence.
Brute Beast Nominal conciousness, exist only to serve beings of higher tiers than them. Little chromosome yield.
The Wretched A creature doomed to a fate of constant agony. The outlaws amongst realms. Those who commited terrible atrocities end here.
